About BMI Calculator

BMI (Body Mass Index) is a calculation used to determine whether your weight is healthy for your height.

BMI Categories

Below 18.5 — Underweight
18.5 to 24.9 — Normal weight
25 to 29.9 — Overweight
30 and above — Obese
