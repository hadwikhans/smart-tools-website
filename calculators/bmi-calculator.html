⚖️ BMI Calculator

About BMI Calculator

BMI (Body Mass Index) is a simple calculation used to determine whether your weight is healthy for your height.

How BMI is Calculated

BMI is calculated using the formula weight divided by height squared. This calculator automatically computes your BMI when you enter your height and weight.
