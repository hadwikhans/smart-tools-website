About Loan EMI Calculator

The Loan EMI Calculator helps you calculate the monthly installment for your loan based on the loan amount, interest rate, and tenure.

How EMI is Calculated

EMI stands for Equated Monthly Installment. It is calculated using a formula that includes loan amount, interest rate, and loan duration.
