📅 Age Calculator

About Age Calculator

The Age Calculator helps you calculate your exact age based on your date of birth. Simply select your birth date and the calculator will instantly show your age in years.

How to Use

1. Select your date of birth.
2. Click the calculate button.
3. Your age will be displayed instantly.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Age Calculator work?

The Age Calculator determines your age by comparing your date of birth with the current date.

Is this age calculator accurate?

Yes. It calculates your age using the selected birth date and the current date.

Can I use this tool for official purposes?

This tool is useful for quick calculations but official documents should be verified with official records.
