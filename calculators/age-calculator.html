About Age Calculator

The Age Calculator helps you calculate your exact age based on your date of birth. Simply select your birth date and the calculator will instantly show your age in years, months and days.

How to Use

1. Select your date of birth.
2. Click the Calculate button.
3. Your age will be displayed instantly.

How does the Age Calculator work?

It compares your date of birth with today's date to calculate your age accurately.

Is this age calculator accurate?

Yes. It calculates your exact age including leap years.
