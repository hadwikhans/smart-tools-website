About GLN, GCP and SGLN

GCP is the GS1 Company Prefix assigned by GS1. GLN is a 13-digit Global Location Number built from the GCP, location reference and GS1 check digit. SGLN is the serialized location identifier derived from the GLN plus an extension component.

For GLN generation, your GCP + Location Reference must equal 12 digits before the final GS1 Mod 10 check digit is added.

Important: a generated GLN can be algorithmically correct and still not appear in Verified by GS1. GS1 verification depends on the prefix and location being actually issued and registered in GS1 data sources.
