About Crop PDF

Trim the margins from your PDF pages by specifying how many points to remove from each side. Useful for removing white borders, headers/footers, or reducing page size. Processing happens entirely in your browser — nothing is uploaded to any server.

Common values

1 inch = 72 points  |  1 cm ≈ 28 points  |  1 mm ≈ 2.8 points
