About OCR PDF

OCR (Optical Character Recognition) reads text from scanned documents and images. Uses Tesseract.js — a powerful open-source OCR engine that runs entirely in your browser. No data is uploaded to any server.

Note: For scanned PDFs, upload the first page as an image (JPG/PNG) for best results. Processing may take 10–30 seconds depending on the document size.
