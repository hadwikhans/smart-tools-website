Privacy Policy

How SmartToolsHub handles website usage and browser-based tool interactions

Overview

SmartToolsHub provides browser-based tools for calculators, developer workflows, text utilities, image handling, PDF tasks, serialization, and SEO-related tasks. We value user privacy and aim to collect as little personal information as reasonably possible.

This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected when you use SmartToolsHub, how it may be used, and how you can contact us regarding privacy concerns.

Information We May Collect

Most SmartToolsHub tools are designed to run directly in your browser. In many cases, the text, files, or content you enter into a tool may never be sent to our servers at all.

We may still collect limited technical information such as browser type, device type, referring pages, approximate location derived from IP address, and standard website usage logs for security, performance, analytics, and troubleshooting.

Tool Inputs and Generated Outputs

Where a tool operates entirely in the browser, your inputs and outputs remain on your device unless you choose to copy, download, or share them yourself.

If any future tool requires server-side processing, storage, uploads, or API integrations, this policy may be updated to reflect that change.

Cookies, Analytics, and Advertising

SmartToolsHub may use cookies or similar technologies for essential site functionality, traffic analysis, user experience improvements, and site security.

We may also use third-party analytics or advertising services in the future, including services such as Google Analytics or Google AdSense. Those services may use cookies or similar technologies subject to their own privacy policies.

How We Use Information

Any limited information collected may be used to operate the website, improve tool performance, measure traffic and engagement, prevent abuse, diagnose technical issues, and support future feature development.

Third-Party Services

Some pages or tools may rely on third-party libraries, fonts, embedded services, APIs, analytics, or advertising partners. SmartToolsHub is not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party websites or services, and users should review those providers separately when relevant.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect website systems and reduce unnecessary data collection. However, no online system, website, or transmission method can be guaranteed fully secure at all times.

Your Choices

You may stop using the website at any time, clear cookies through your browser settings, and choose not to submit any information through interactive features or contact channels.

Children's Privacy

SmartToolsHub is not intentionally directed toward children under the age of 13. If you believe that personal information from a child has been provided to us unintentionally, please contact us so that we can review the issue.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect site improvements, new tools, changes in applicable law, analytics changes, or monetization updates. The revised version will be posted on this page.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, please contact us at supportsmarttools@gmail.com.
